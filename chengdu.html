Chengdu J-20 Stealth Fighter

Overview

The Chengdu J-20 is China's fifth-generation stealth fighter jet. Designed for air superiority, it combines stealth, long-range strike, and advanced avionics.

Key Features

Role: Stealth air superiority fighter
Engines: Twin turbofan engines with supercruise capability
Weapons: Internal weapons bay for radar stealth
Avionics: AESA radar, advanced electronic warfare systems
