Dongfeng Warrior Utility Vehicle

Overview

The Dongfeng Warrior is a Chinese high-mobility multi-purpose vehicle (HMMWV equivalent). It is used for troop transport, reconnaissance, and logistics.

Key Features

Mobility: High off-road performance, all-terrain capability
Capacity: Can carry personnel, weapons, or equipment
Variants: Reconnaissance, troop carrier, command post
Role: Tactical transport and light combat support
