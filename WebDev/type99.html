Type 99 Main Battle Tank

Overview

The Type 99 (ZTZ-99) is China's most advanced third-generation main battle tank (MBT). It features a 125mm smoothbore gun with anti-tank missile capability, a 1200 hp diesel engine, and advanced armor protection including Explosive Reactive Armor (ERA).

Key Features

Armament: 125mm ZPT-98 smoothbore gun, ATGM-capable
Protection: Composite armor + ERA
Mobility: 1200 hp engine, high power-to-weight ratio
Systems: JD-3 laser dazzler, fire control with thermal imaging, autoloader (8 rpm)
