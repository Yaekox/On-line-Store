ZBD-04 Infantry Fighting Vehicle

Overview

The ZBD-04 is a modern Chinese infantry fighting vehicle (IFV) designed to transport troops while providing fire support on the battlefield. It is amphibious and highly mobile.

Key Features

Armament: 100mm rifled gun, 30mm autocannon, 7.62mm machine gun
Mobility: Amphibious capability, good cross-country performance
Capacity: Can carry up to 7 troops
Role: Mechanized infantry support and firepower
